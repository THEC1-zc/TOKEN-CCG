üìñ How to Play
1. Get Cards
Use Deck Minter to generate 10 cards quickly, or Card Minter for custom cards.
2. Build Battle Deck
Go to Deck Builder. Create 2 decks of 10 cards each (different Houses).
3. Play!
Select your Battle Deck and play vs AI. Capture cards, make TOKENs, win!
4. Scoring
Points for: most cards, TOKENs (clearing table), special cards (12-15), and Aces.
